Woven Artistry Meets Industrial Precision

Jacquard weaving is one of the most sophisticated textile manufacturing techniques, allowing for complex, multi-color patterns to be woven directly into the ribbon structure — rather than printed on the surface.

Unlike printed ribbons, jacquard patterns are permanent, fray-resistant, and maintain their beauty even after washing. The raised texture creates a tactile, premium quality that conveys luxury and craftsmanship.

Our jacquard looms produce ribbons from 10mm to 150mm wide, with up to 16-color pattern capability.

Jacquard Ribbon Types & Applications

Jacquard ribbons for every industry and creative need

🏷️ Logo & Brand Jacquard

Your company logo or brand pattern woven in vibrant, durable colors. Ideal for apparel labels, corporate gift ribbons, and premium brand packaging. Permanent, wash-resistant branding.

🌸 Floral & Ornamental Jacquard

Traditional and contemporary ornamental patterns — scrolls, florals, geometric motifs — woven into ribbon. Popular for wedding décor, craft ribbons, and premium packaging.

🧵 Grosgrain Jacquard

Combines the durability of grosgrain with the beauty of jacquard patterns. Textured, ribbed surface with intricate woven designs. Perfect for apparel and heavy-use applications.

🎀 Satin Jacquard

Luxurious satin base with jacquard woven patterns. The glossy surface combined with woven texture creates an exceptionally premium ribbon for luxury gift and fashion applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between jacquard and printed ribbons?

Jacquard ribbons have patterns woven directly into the fabric structure. The pattern is integral to the ribbon — it cannot be scratched off, washed away, or faded by sunlight. Printed ribbons have designs applied to the surface.

What is the minimum order quantity for custom jacquard ribbons?

Custom jacquard MOQ starts at 500 meters. For complex multi-color patterns, the MOQ may be 1,000 meters. Stock jacquard patterns can be ordered in smaller quantities — please inquire.

How long does it take to develop a custom jacquard pattern?

Pattern development (from your artwork to production-ready weave design) typically takes 7–10 working days. We provide a digital weave simulation for your approval before producing samples.

Can jacquard ribbons be used outdoors?

Yes, UV-resistant polyester jacquard ribbons are suitable for outdoor use. They maintain colorfastness under sun exposure. We also offer waterproof-treated options for marine and outdoor applications.

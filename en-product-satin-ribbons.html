The Gold Standard of Ribbons

Satin ribbon is the most versatile and widely-used ribbon type in the world. Its smooth, glossy surface reflects light beautifully, creating a premium look that elevates any application.

Our satin ribbons are woven from 100% polyester filaments (or silk blends for luxury grades), offering excellent draping quality, color richness, and durability. Available in widths from 3mm to 150mm.

Whether you need a few hundred meters for a craft project or 50,000 meters for retail packaging, our production lines can fulfill orders of any scale with consistent quality.

Satin Ribbon Types

Choose the right satin ribbon for your application

💎 Double-Faced Satin

Glossy on both sides — the premium choice for visible applications. Ideal for luxury gift wrapping, wedding décor, and fashion accessories. Our best-selling product category.

🌟 Single-Faced Satin

Glossy on the front, matte on the back. Cost-effective alternative for applications where only one side is visible. Widely used in apparel and craft projects.

🎨 Printed Satin

Custom designs, logos, or patterns printed on satin base. Perfect for brand packaging, holiday promotions, and personalized gift ribbons.

🧵 Double-Sided Satin (Silk-Blend)

Premium silk-polyester blend for luxury applications. Exceptional drape and sheen. Preferred by high-end fashion houses and luxury gift brands.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between double-faced and single-faced satin?

Double-faced satin has a glossy, smooth surface on both sides, making it ideal for applications where the ribbon is visible from multiple angles. Single-faced satin is glossy on one side and matte on the other — a cost-effective solution for one-sided applications.

Can you match a specific color from a sample?

Yes. We can match colors from physical samples, Pantone (PMS) codes, RAL numbers, or digital references. Our in-house dye lab produces color samples within 3–5 working days.

What is your minimum order quantity?

Standard MOQ is 500 meters per color and width. For stock colors, smaller quantities (100m+) can be arranged at slightly higher unit prices.

Do you offer printed satin ribbons with custom designs?

Absolutely. We offer flexo, screen, and heat-transfer printing on satin ribbons. MOQ starts at 500 meters per design. Our design team can help optimize your artwork.

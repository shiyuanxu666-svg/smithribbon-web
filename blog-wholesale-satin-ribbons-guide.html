2. Key Factors When Choosing a Wholesale Satin Ribbon Supplier

Not all Chinese satin ribbon suppliers are created equal. Before placing an order, evaluate these critical factors:

🏭 Factory Verification

Always verify that your supplier operates a genuine manufacturing facility, not just a trading company. Ask for:

  • Business license and export license copies
  • Photos or video tours of the production line
  • Factory floor size and employee count
  • Third-party inspection reports (SGS, Bureau Veritas)

📦 Production Capacity

For large orders, ensure the factory can meet your volume requirements. At Smith Ribbon, our 15,000㎡ facility in Xiamen produces 100,000+ meters of ribbon daily, ensuring consistent supply even for the largest orders.

🎨 Customization Capability

The best suppliers offer comprehensive customization:

  • Custom logo printing (screen print, heat transfer, flexo)
  • Custom colors matching brand Pantone codes
  • Custom widths from 3mm to 150mm
  • Custom packaging with branded labels and cards

5. Minimum Order Quantities Explained

Most Chinese satin ribbon manufacturers set MOQs to ensure production efficiency. Here's what to expect:

  • Standard satin ribbon (solid colors): 500–1,000 meters per color
  • Custom printed satin ribbon: 1,000–3,000 meters per design
  • Fully custom (logo + color + width): 2,000–5,000 meters
  • Sample orders: 10–50 meters (available at higher unit price)
💡 Pro Tip: Combine Colors to Meet MOQ If your order doesn't meet the MOQ for a single color, ask your supplier if you can split the production run across multiple colors. Many factories offer this flexibility at no extra charge.

6. Red Flags to Watch Out For

⚠️ Warning Signs When Vetting Suppliers
  • Refuses to share factory photos or video calls
  • Price is significantly lower than market rate (usually too good to be true)
  • No verifiable business license or export documentation
  • Communicates only via personal email, no company domain
  • Pressure tactics demanding full payment upfront with no contract
  • No physical sample available before bulk production

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for custom printed ribbons?

Our standard MOQ is 500 meters per design. For repeat orders, we offer flexible MOQ options starting from 300 meters. Larger orders receive progressively better unit pricing.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes. We offer pre-production samples (5–7 days) so you can verify print quality, color accuracy, and material feel before committing to full production.

What file formats do you accept for custom designs?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. Our design team can also help adapt your existing artwork to ribbon printing specifications.

What printing methods do you offer?

Flexo printing (best for large runs), screen printing (excellent color vibrancy), offset printing (photorealistic quality), and heat transfer (complex multi-color designs).

How do you match colors to my brand?

We use Pantone (PMS) color matching for accuracy. Please provide your PMS color codes or send a physical color reference. We also provide digital color proofs before production.

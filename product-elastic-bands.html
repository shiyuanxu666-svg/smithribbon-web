产品介绍

Product Introduction

松紧带是具有弹性的功能性织带产品。厦门思蜜丝织带采用优质 橡胶丝和化纤丝组合织造，生产出回弹力强、耐久性好、 宽度稳定的松紧带产品。我们的松紧带具有弹性持久不变形、 耐老化、耐洗涤、手感舒适等特点，广泛应用于服装腰口、 袖口、裤口、医用敷料、运动护具、箱包收口等领域。 我们提供多种规格、多种弹力级别的松紧带现货供应， 同时支持特殊规格定制。

Elastic band is a functional ribbon product with elasticity. Xiamen Smith Ribbon uses high-quality rubber threads and chemical fiber threads to produce elastic bands with strong resilience, good durability, and stable width. Our elastic bands have characteristics of permanent elasticity without deformation, aging resistance, wash resistance, comfortable texture, widely used in garment waist, cuffs, pant legs, medical dressings, sports protective gear, bag openings, and other fields. We provide various specifications and elasticity levels of elastic band stock, while supporting custom specifications.

  • 优质橡胶丝和氨纶丝，弹性持久不变形
  • High-quality rubber and spandex threads, permanent elasticity without deformation
  • 回弹力强，反复拉伸不松弛
  • Strong resilience, no relaxation after repeated stretching
  • 耐老化耐洗涤，使用寿命长
  • Aging and wash resistance, long service life
  • 多种弹力级别可选：轻度、中度、高度
  • Multiple elasticity levels available: light, medium, high
  • 宽度稳定，不易卷边
  • Stable width, not easy to curl
  • 可与各种面料缝制结合
  • Can be sewn and combined with various fabrics

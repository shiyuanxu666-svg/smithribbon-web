Bow Types We Manufacture

Every bow style — ready to customize for your brand

🎀 Pre-Tied Bows

Professionally crafted, ready-to-apply bows on wires or alligator clips. Consistent shape and size for retail and packaging line use. Available in satin, grosgrain, velvet, and jacquard ribbons.

🎈 Pull Bows

Fan-folded ribbon bows that "bloom" when pulled. The most popular retail bow type — fast to apply, dramatic in appearance. Available in glossy, matte, velvet, and glitter finishes.

🔴 Curly Ribbon Bows

Made from precut curly ribbon loops. Lightweight, vibrant, and festive. A retail bestseller, especially in Christmas colors. Ideal for high-volume packaging operations.

🎄 Christmas Bows

Our largest bow category. Indoor/outdoor-rated Christmas bows in traditional red/green, elegant gold/cream, and modern color combinations. Wire-edged for shape retention.

Frequently Asked Questions

What bow size should I choose for my application?

As a general guide: 5–7cm for favor boxes and small gifts; 10–13cm for standard gift boxes; 15–20cm for large gift boxes and wine bottles; 25cm+ for hampers, wreaths, and automotive displays. When in doubt, ask our team — we offer free consultations.

Can you make bows matching our brand colors?

Yes. We match to Pantone (PMS), RAL, or physical samples. For repeat orders, we create a proprietary color standard that is archived for future production — ensuring color consistency across every batch.

What is the difference between pull bows and pre-tied bows?

Pull bows come flat (fan-folded) and expand when you pull the strings — they "bloom" into a full bow shape. Pre-tied bows arrive already formed and just need to be placed and secured.

Can you print our logo on the bows?

Yes. We offer screen printing and heat transfer printing on pre-tied bows and medal ribbons. MOQ is 500 pieces per design. For larger orders, engraved or woven logo bows are also available.

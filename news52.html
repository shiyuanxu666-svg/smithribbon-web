Smith Ribbon 2026年可持续发展报告：再生涤纶织带产量突破500万米，碳足迹降低40%

Smith Ribbon 2026 Sustainability Report: RPET Ribbon Output Exceeds 5M Meters, Carbon Footprint Down 40%
📅 2026-04-10📁 公司动态 / Company News🏭 Smith Ribbon

文章摘要 Abstract

Smith Ribbon's 2026 Sustainability Report documents significant environmental milestones achieved through our commitment to sustainable ribbon manufacturing. Key achievements in 2025-2026 include: RPET (recycled polyester) ribbon production exceeding 5 million meters — a 250% increase from 2023 levels — representing 28% of total production volume. Carbon footprint per meter of ribbon produced has been reduced by 40% compared to our 2020 baseline, driven by a 35% shift to renewable energy in our Xiamen factory and a 60% reduction in water consumption through closed-loop recycling systems. Smith Ribbon has successfully obtained GRS (Global Recycled Standard) certification for our RPET product line, complementing existing OEKO-TEX Standard 100, FSC, and BSCI certifications. Our 2026 sustainability roadmap targets 40% RPET ratio by volume and a 60% carbon footprint reduction versus the 2020 baseline. We are proud to partner with global brands committed to sustainable packaging, including several Fortune 500 consumer goods companies and leading European fashion retailers. Smith Ribbon remains dedicated to proving that premium-quality ribbon manufacturing and environmental responsibility can — and must — coexist.

一、RPET再生涤纶织带进展 RPET Ribbon Progress

RPET（再生涤纶纤维）织带是Smith Ribbon可持续发展的核心产品线。RPET纤维来源于回收的PET塑料瓶，每吨RPET纤维可消耗约50,000个塑料瓶，显著减少石油消耗和塑料废弃物。

RPET (Recycled Polyester) ribbon is the cornerstone of Smith Ribbon's sustainability strategy. Each ton of RPET fiber consumes approximately 50,000 recycled PET bottles, significantly reducing petroleum consumption and plastic waste.

  • 2025年RPET织带产量: 超过500万米，较2023年增长250%
  • RPET占总产量比例: 2025年达到28%，目标2026年提升至40%
  • RPET产品规格: 已覆盖缎带、斜纹带、提花织带、欧根纱织带等全品类
  • 质量认证: 所有RPET产品均通过OEKO-TEX Standard 100及GRS（全球回收标准）认证
🌿 RPET环境效益 / RPET Environmental Benefits:
• 每生产100万米RPET织带，消耗约400吨回收PET塑料瓶
• 相比原生涤纶，RPET减少约65%的碳排放
• 相比原生涤纶，RPET降低约70%的能源消耗
• 减少塑料进入海洋和土壤的污染风险

二、碳减排数据与清洁能源 Carbon Reduction & Clean Energy

Smith Ribbon持续加大清洁能源投入，2025年工厂能源消耗中35%来自太阳能和绿色电力，较2020年提升20个百分点。通过设备升级、能效优化和绿色电力采购三管齐下，单位产品碳足迹较2020年基准线降低了40%。

  • 碳足迹核算: 已建立基于ISO 14067标准的产品碳足迹核算体系
  • 清洁能源: 15,000㎡工厂屋顶光伏项目装机容量500kW，年发电量约60万度
  • 碳减排路线图: 2026年目标降低50%，2028年目标降低65%（vs 2020基准）
  • 碳中和承诺: 计划2030年实现范围一和范围二碳中和

三、ESG认证进展 ESG Certification Progress

Smith Ribbon已建立全面的ESG认证体系，涵盖环境保护、社会责任和公司治理三个维度：

  • GRS全球回收标准: 2025年成功获得GRS认证，覆盖RPET织带全产品线
  • OEKO-TEX Standard 100: 持续保持，所有产品均通过有害物质检测
  • FSC森林认证: 确保用于织带生产的木浆和纤维素纤维来源于可持续管理森林
  • BSCI/SEDEX: 持续通过社会责任审核，保障工人权益
  • ISO 14001: 环境管理体系认证工厂全流程覆盖
  • ISO 9001: 质量管理体系持续有效运行

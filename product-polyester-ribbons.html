产品介绍

Product Introduction

涤纶织带是市场上应用最广泛的织带产品之一。厦门思蜜丝织带选用优质 100%涤纶纤维，采用先进的织造设备和技术，生产出品质稳定、价格合理 的涤纶织带产品。涤纶织带具有强度高、耐磨损、耐腐蚀、易清洗、 快干等优点，广泛应用于服装辅料、工业配件、包装捆扎、工艺品制作 等多个领域。我们提供多种规格、多种颜色的涤纶织带现货供应， 同时支持定制生产，满足不同客户的个性化需求。

Polyester ribbon is one of the most widely used ribbon products in the market. Xiamen Smith Ribbon selects high-quality 100% polyester fibers, uses advanced weaving equipment and technology to produce quality-stable, reasonably-priced polyester ribbon products. Polyester ribbon has advantages of high strength, wear resistance, corrosion resistance, easy cleaning, quick drying, etc., widely used in garment accessories, industrial accessories, packaging bundling, craft making and other fields. We provide various specifications and colors of polyester ribbon stock, while supporting custom production to meet individual needs of different customers.

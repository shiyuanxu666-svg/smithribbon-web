The Case for Chinese Ribbon Factories in 2026

Despite rising labor costs and geopolitical headwinds, China still holds decisive structural advantages in ribbon and bow manufacturing. For Southeast Asian buyers targeting quality, variety, and competitive pricing, China remains the dominant supply base.

尽管劳动力成本上升和地缘政治压力，中国在织带和蝴蝶结制造领域仍拥有决定性的结构性优势。对于追求质量、品种多样性和有竞争力价格的东南亚买家来说，中国仍然是主要的采购基地。

Key Data: China produces approximately 65% of the world's narrow fabric ribbons and decorative bows. The Xiamen-Putian corridor alone accounts for over 40% of China's ribbon export volume, with 20+ years of specialized supply chain clustering.

关键数据：中国生产了全球约65%的窄幅织带和装饰蝴蝶结。仅厦门-莆田走廊就占中国织带出口量的40%以上，拥有20多年的专业供应链集聚。

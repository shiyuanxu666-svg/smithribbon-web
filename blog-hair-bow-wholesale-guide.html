Key Specifications When Sourcing Hair Bows

Before placing an order with a Chinese hair bow supplier, clarify these specifications in writing:

  1. Width & Size — measured in inches or cm. Common sizes: 1.5" (kids), 2.5" (toddlers), 3.5" (girls), 4"+ (adults)
  2. Clip Type — alligator clip (standard), snap clip, French clip, pigtail ring
  3. Base Material — alligator clip, elastic band, barette clip
  4. Adhesive — glue-sticked bows (standard) vs. sewn bows (more durable, higher cost)
  5. Age Range — 0-3 years requires softer elastics and rounded edges
  6. Safety Standards — for US market: CPSC requirements; for EU: EN 71 toy safety standard
  7. Packaging — bulk polybag (standard) vs. individual carded packaging (retail-ready)

How to Find a Reliable Hair Bow Supplier in China

The hair bow market is full of trading companies posing as factories. Here's how to verify authenticity:

  1. Request a factory video walkthrough — real factories will provide this; trading companies typically won't
  2. Check the business license — confirm manufacturing scope on the Chinese business registration
  3. Ask for OEKO-TEX certificate — verify the certificate number in the OEKO-TEX database
  4. Request physical samples first — always evaluate quality before bulk orders
  5. Check export history — reliable suppliers export to your target market already
  6. Negotiate a small trial order first — 200–500 pieces to test quality and communication

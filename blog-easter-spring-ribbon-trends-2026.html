Trending Ribbon Styles This Season

1. Botanical & Floral Printed Ribbons

Floral motifs — cherry blossoms, peonies, wildflowers — are dominating spring collections in 2026. These printed grosgrain ribbons are a top seller for gift wrapping brands and artisan craft retailers.

MOQ: 1,000 meters | Lead time: 7-14 days

2. Easter Pull Bows & Pre-Made Bows

Ready-to-use Easter pull bows in pastel colors are in massive demand from January through April. Retailers love them for their ease — no tying required, just pull and attach.

Top colors: Blush pink, soft yellow, mint green

3. Eco-Friendly & Recycled Ribbons

Sustainability is no longer optional. RPET (recycled polyester) ribbons and natural jute ribbons are gaining traction with eco-conscious brands targeting European and North American markets.

Certifications that sell: OEKO-TEX, GRS, FSC

4. Iridescent & Shimmer Ribbons

Shimmering, color-shifting ribbons are trending for premium gift packaging. Often used in metallic organza or holographic grosgrain, these ribbons command higher retail prices.

5. Velvet Ribbons for Luxury Spring Collections

Deep jewel tones (emerald, burgundy) in velvet are being mixed with spring pastels in luxury gift sets and perfume packaging.

Wholesale Sourcing Checklist for Spring 2026

  • Stock availability: Confirm materials are in stock, not on backorder
  • Lead time buffer: Factor in 2-3 weeks production + 2-4 weeks shipping
  • Custom colors: PMS color matching available (minimum 3,000m for custom dye)
  • Sample approval: Always request pre-production samples for new colors
  • Certifications: OEKO-TEX for Europe, CPSIA compliance for USA
  • Eco claims: Ensure third-party certificates before marketing as "sustainable"

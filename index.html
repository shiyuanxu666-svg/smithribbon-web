厦门思蜜丝织带饰品有限公司

坐落于厦门市同安区美溪道同安工业园16号楼，是一家集设计、生产、销售于一体的专业织带制造商，专注礼品包装、圣诞装饰、家居配饰领域深耕20年。工厂占地15000㎡，拥有200名专业员工，通过SEDEX验厂认证。

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from global buyers about sourcing ribbons from China

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ is 500 meters per design/color for standard ribbons. For custom printed or jacquard ribbons, MOQ starts at 1,000 meters. We offer small batch orders (500m minimum) for selected products. Sample orders are welcome.

Do you offer OEM and custom logo printing?

Yes, we specialize in OEM and ODM services. Custom logo printing, custom colors (Pantone matching), and custom woven patterns (jacquard) are all available. Our MOQ for custom work starts at 1,000 meters with a 7-14 day sample lead time.

What certifications do you hold?

We hold OEKO-TEX Standard 100, ISO 9001, FSC, BSCI, SEDEX, and SMETA certifications. All certificates are available for download on our website and can be verified with the respective certification bodies.

What is your production lead time?

Standard stock orders ship within 7 days. Custom printed or jacquard orders require 15-25 working days for production. Rush orders are available with a 20-30 percent surcharge. Sea freight to US West Coast takes 20-25 days; air freight 5-7 days.

Do you ship globally?

Yes, we export to 50+ countries including USA, UK, Germany, France, Australia, Japan, and more. We work with reliable freight forwarders and offer FOB, CIF, and DDP shipping terms. All shipments include full documentation (invoice, packing list, COO, BL).

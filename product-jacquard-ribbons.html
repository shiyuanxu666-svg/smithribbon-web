产品介绍

Product Introduction

提花织带是采用先进提花织造工艺织造的高档织带产品。 厦门思蜜丝织带拥有多台高端提花织机，能够织造各种复杂图案和LOGO。 提花织带的图案是织入织带纤维中的立体结构，比印刷图案更加牢固耐用， 不会因摩擦或洗涤而褪色脱落。我们的提花织带广泛应用于服装商标、 箱包配件、鞋材辅料、工艺品装饰等领域，是品牌标识和高端产品配套的理想选择。

Jacquard ribbon is a high-grade ribbon product woven using advanced jacquard weaving technology. Xiamen Smith Ribbon has multiple high-end jacquard looms capable of weaving various complex patterns and LOGO. The patterns on jacquard ribbon are three-dimensional structures woven into the ribbon fibers, more durable than printed patterns, and won't fade or peel off due to friction or washing. Our jacquard ribbons are widely used in garment labels, bag accessories, shoe materials, craft decorations, and other fields, making them ideal for brand marking and high-end product matching.

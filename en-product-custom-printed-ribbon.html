Why Custom Printed Ribbon is the Ultimate Brand Touchpoint

Every gift package you send is a brand moment. The ribbon on your package is the first and last thing your customer touches — and it's often the most photographed element of an unboxing. Custom printed ribbon transforms ordinary packaging into a powerful brand statement.

Custom Ribbon Types Available

Custom Logo Satin Ribbon

Your brand logo printed repeatedly along the length of satin ribbon — the most popular choice for premium retail packaging. The smooth, glossy surface of satin showcases print quality beautifully. Available in single-face and double-face satin.

Custom Pattern Grosgrain Ribbon

Custom patterns on grosgrain ribbon — the ribbed texture adds visual interest and depth to printed designs. Grosgrain is particularly popular for gift packaging, bakery boxes, and premium consumer goods.

Custom Velvet Ribbon

Luxurious velvet ribbon with your brand logo or pattern — a premium choice for high-end cosmetics, jewelry, and luxury gifting. The soft texture of velvet combined with crisp printing creates an exceptional luxury experience.

Custom Organza Ribbon

Sheer organza ribbon with custom prints — elegant and delicate, ideal for wedding favors, floral arrangements, and feminine brands. The semi-transparent quality creates unique visual effects when printed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What format does my logo/design need to be in?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, or high-resolution PNG/JPEG (minimum 300dpi at actual size). Vector files (AI/EPS) are preferred as they allow the best print quality and color matching. Our design team can also help optimize your artwork at no extra cost.

Q: Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Yes. We offer sample orders of 30-50m so you can physically assess print quality, color accuracy, and material feel before committing to full production. Sample fee is deducted from bulk order. Samples ship within 10-15 days.

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for custom printed ribbon?

Standard MOQ: 1,000m per design. Digital printing: MOQ 200m per design. For large-volume orders (10,000m+), flexo printing offers the best per-meter cost. We work with brands of all sizes — contact us to discuss your volume requirements.

Q: How long does it take from design approval to bulk delivery?

Design mockup: 5 business days. Physical sample: 10-15 days. Bulk production: 15-25 days after sample approval. Total lead time: 4-6 weeks from design approval. Rush orders available with a 15-20% surcharge for priority scheduling.

Q: Can I print in multiple colors on one ribbon?

Yes. Screen printing supports up to 8 colors. Heat transfer and digital printing support full-color photographic prints. Each additional color requires a separate screen and setup — talk to us about optimizing your design for both visual impact and cost efficiency.

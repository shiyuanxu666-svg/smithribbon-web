Why visit our factory before placing a bulk order?

For B2B buyers sourcing custom ribbons from China, the factory is the product. A beautiful website doesn't tell you whether the factory can deliver 50,000m of custom printed satin ribbons on time with consistent Pantone matching. This page gives you a transparent look at the actual facility — the same factory you'll ship from.

1. Showroom: 200+ stock samples in one room

When you visit (or video-call our sales team), the first stop is our showroom. Every material we manufacture has a hanging sample: satin, grosgrain, organza, velvet, chiffon, burlap, RPET recycled, bamboo fiber, paper, metallic-edged, glitter, flocked, wired-edge, fold-over elastic — in every standard Pantone color we stock. This is also where you can mix and match textures for your brand's signature look.

2. Christmas Decor Showroom: Q4 ready

For Q4 buyers (retailers, gift packagers, e-commerce sellers), we maintain a dedicated Christmas decor showroom showcasing wired edge ribbons in classic red/green/gold/silver, finished bows, pull bows, tree toppers, and pre-tied bows ready for sampling. Photos update every June for the upcoming Q4 cycle.

3. Digital Printing Workshop: 24h turnaround on custom logos

Our digital printing workshop is the core of our custom logo ribbon service. The 2-meter-wide digital printer shown here handles small-batch custom orders (50–5,000m) with no plate fee and Pantone-matched color profiles. Lead time for samples: 3–5 days. This is also where we do quick-turn private-label poly bag printing.

4. Canton Fair 2025: We exhibited at Hall V05A

Smith Ribbon exhibited at Canton Fair 2025 (Spring session), Hall V05A — one of the highest-traffic textile halls. This is our third consecutive year exhibiting. Buyers from Walmart, Target, L'Oréal, Carrefour, and 1,000+ smaller B2B brands have visited our booth for in-person sample inspection. If you're attending the next Canton Fair, our booth is in the same hall — we update the location on our homepage before each session.

5. Sample Wall: B2B clients' finished products

This is the wall of our most-shipped B2B products. Each sample is a finished product we've delivered to clients — pre-tied bows, gift bows, wired-edge Christmas tree ties, foil-stamped "Happy Birthday" ribbons, organza flower wraps. For new buyers, this is the best starting point: pick from proven designs, send us your logo, and we can ship samples within 5 days.

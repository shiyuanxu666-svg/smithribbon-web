What is Jacquard Ribbon?

Jacquard ribbon is a type of woven ribbon with intricate patterns created using a Jacquard loom — a device that controls individual warp threads to produce complex designs. Unlike printed ribbons where patterns are applied to the surface, jacquard patterns are woven into the fabric itself, creating a durable, three-dimensional texture that lasts the lifetime of the product.

Smith Ribbon's jacquard ribbons are widely used by luxury fashion brands, premium perfume packaging, high-end jewelry boxes, designer gift wraps, and upscale home décor collections. The woven construction ensures the pattern never fades, peels, or rubs off — even after washing or heavy use.

Why Choose Woven Jacquard Over Printed Patterns?

  • Durability: Woven patterns are integral to the fabric — they never fade, peel, or scratch off. Printed patterns degrade over time with washing and friction.
  • Luxury Perception: The three-dimensional texture and weight of jacquard conveys quality that surface printing cannot replicate.
  • Design Complexity: Jacquard weaving can reproduce photograph-quality illustrations with precise color gradations and intricate detail.
  • Branding Impact: A woven logo on a ribbon is a statement of brand permanence and quality — exactly what luxury brands communicate.
  • Versatility: Available in virtually any pattern, color combination, and width. From minimalist single-color geometrics to full-color photographic florals.

Q: What widths are available for jacquard ribbon?

Standard widths: 1cm, 1.5cm, 2cm, 2.5cm, 3cm, 4cm, 5cm. Custom widths available from 0.5cm to 10cm — specify your requirements when requesting a quote.

Q: Are your jacquard ribbons washable?

Yes. Since the pattern is woven into the fabric (not printed on the surface), jacquard ribbons maintain their appearance even after hand washing or machine gentle cycle. Do not bleach. Air dry away from direct sunlight for best color retention.

Q: Do you provide OEKO-TEX certification for jacquard ribbons?

Yes. Smith Ribbon holds OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification. All our jacquard ribbons are tested and certified free from harmful substances — safe for use in direct-skin contact applications and children's products. Certificate provided with each shipment.

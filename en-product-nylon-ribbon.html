What is Nylon Ribbon?

Nylon ribbon is a versatile, durable ribbon made from 100% nylon fibers. Known for its exceptional softness, high tensile strength, and resistance to abrasion and fraying, nylon ribbon is a top choice for applications ranging from garment manufacturing to luxury hair accessories.

Smith Ribbon's nylon ribbons are woven in our Xiamen factory using premium nylon yarn, ensuring consistent quality, color accuracy, and durability across every production run. Whether you need 500m of stock colors or 10,000m of custom-matched colors, we deliver the quality and consistency your brand demands.

Nylon Ribbon Types

Nylon Grosgrain Ribbon

Characterized by its distinctive ribbed texture (vertical ridges), grosgrain is one of the most versatile ribbon types available. The ridges provide excellent grip, making grosgrain ribbons ideal for applications where the ribbon needs to stay in place — such as hair bows, straps, and tied gifts.

  • Soft matte finish with a premium feel
  • Excellent grip and knot-holding properties
  • Available in 80+ stock colors
  • Washable and fray-resistant when cut with hot knife
  • Custom widths from 1cm to 10cm

Nylon Herringbone Ribbon

Herringbone weave creates a distinctive V-shaped zigzag pattern. This weave is particularly valued in fashion and luxury applications for its visual sophistication and texture.

  • Elegant zigzag pattern with premium texture
  • Highly durable and colorfast
  • Popular for belt accents, strap details, and luxury fashion applications
  • Custom colors and patterns available

Nylon Twill Ribbon

Twill weave produces a diagonal pattern. Widely used in apparel manufacturing, particularly for labels, tags, and decorative seams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can nylon ribbon be used in washing machines?

Yes. Nylon ribbon is machine washable. We recommend a gentle cycle with cold or warm water. Avoid bleach and high heat drying. Nylon dries quickly. For best results, cut ribbon ends with a hot knife to prevent fraying.

Q: What is the difference between nylon grosgrain and polyester grosgrain?

Nylon grosgrain is softer and has higher tensile strength — ideal for garments, hair accessories, and straps. Polyester grosgrain is more colorfast and offers better resistance to UV exposure — ideal for outdoor applications and printed ribbons. Both are durable and washable.

Q: Can I get custom widths of nylon ribbon?

Yes. We produce custom-width nylon ribbons from 0.5cm to 10cm. Custom widths require a custom production run — MOQ 1,000m per width. Lead time: 15-25 days.

Q: Do you offer sublimation printing on nylon ribbon?

Yes. Nylon takes sublimation printing well, producing vibrant, wash-resistant prints. This is an excellent option for custom patterns and photographic designs on nylon ribbon. MOQ for sublimation-printed nylon ribbon: 1,000m.

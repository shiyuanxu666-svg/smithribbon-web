Why Polyester Satin Ribbon is the #1 Choice for Wholesale Buyers

Polyester satin ribbon is the world's most popular ribbon type — and for good reason. It offers an exceptional combination of visual appeal, durability, versatility, and cost efficiency that no other ribbon material matches.

💡 Why wholesale buyers choose polyester satin: Polyester provides excellent color depth and a luxurious smooth finish at a fraction of the cost of silk satin. It takes all printing methods beautifully, is colorfast, washable, and maintains its appearance over time. For gift wrapping, apparel, and promotional packaging — polyester satin delivers maximum perceived value at wholesale prices.

Available Types

Polyester Satin Ribbon

Smooth, glossy surface with a luxurious sheen. The most versatile ribbon type — suitable for every application from gift wrapping to fashion. Available in both single-face satin (shiny on one side, matte on the other) and double-face satin (glossy on both sides).

Polyester Grosgrain Ribbon

Distinctive ribbed texture. More matte and subtle than satin. Excellent for applications requiring grip and durability — hair bows, straps, craft projects, and children's products.

Custom Printed Polyester Ribbon

Your design, pattern, or logo printed directly on polyester ribbon. Four printing methods available: Screen printing (best for bold designs), Heat transfer (photographic quality), Flexo printing (cost-effective for long runs), Digital printing (best for short runs and color gradients).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the minimum order quantity for polyester ribbon?

Stock colors: MOQ 500m per color. Custom printed ribbons (your design): MOQ 1,000m per design. We also offer small sample orders of 30-50m for quality assessment before committing to bulk orders.

Q: Can polyester satin ribbon be printed with our company logo?

Yes. We offer four printing methods: screen printing (best for bold, solid-color logos), heat transfer (photographic quality), flexo printing (cost-effective for large runs), and digital printing (best for short runs with complex gradients). MOQ 1,000m for custom logo printing.

Q: Is polyester satin ribbon colorfast?

Yes. Our polyester ribbons are dyed using dispersion dyes that are permanently bonded to the fiber — they will not bleed, fade, or transfer color. Polyester is one of the most colorfast textile materials available. For dark colors, we recommend testing on a small sample first.

Q: What widths do you offer for polyester satin ribbon?

Standard widths: 1cm, 1.5cm, 2cm, 2.5cm, 3cm, 4cm, 5cm, 7cm, 10cm. Custom widths from 0.5cm to 30cm available with custom production runs. We also offer standard roll lengths of 50 yards (45.7m) and 100 yards (91.4m).
